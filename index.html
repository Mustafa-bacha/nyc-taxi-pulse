Project Overview

NYC Taxi Pulse is a production-grade interactive dashboard analyzing urban mobility patterns in New York City using Yellow Taxi trip data. Built with Plotly Dash and Python, the dashboard features advanced temporal visualizations, geospatial analysis, and bidirectional brushing & linking across multiple dimensions.

Users can explore 50,000+ taxi trips across January-March 2023, discover patterns in urban mobility, understand weather impacts, analyze revenue distribution, and gain actionable insights into NYC's transportation ecosystem.
